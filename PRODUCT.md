# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Systify is for engineers, maintainers, and technical reviewers who need to understand unfamiliar repositories quickly. They are usually in an authenticated product workflow: importing a GitHub repository, reviewing generated knowledge, asking grounded questions, or producing reusable repository guide artifacts.

## Product Purpose

Systify turns repository source and metadata into cited, reusable analysis. Success means users can trust where an answer came from, move between chat and library artifacts without losing context, and start deeper sandbox-backed analysis only when the workflow calls for it.

## Brand Personality

Precise, technical, and calm. The interface should feel like an engineering tool for repeated use: dense enough to be efficient, quiet enough to stay out of the way, and explicit about state, provenance, and failure.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style SaaS gloss inside app workflows, oversized decorative cards, vague AI assistant chrome, and controls that hide cost, grounding, or model state. Avoid visual novelty that makes standard product interactions harder to scan.

## Design Principles

- Ground trust in visible source, model, cache, and job state.
- Prefer stable, predictable layouts over animated or shifting surfaces.
- Keep controls compact but readable; density should not mean tiny critical labels.
- Make expensive or long-running actions explicit before they start.
- Preserve the existing sharp, technical design system unless a workflow clearly needs a softer container.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target accessible product UI with keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, readable contrast in both light and dark themes, and no reliance on motion to understand state. Reduced-motion preferences should be respected for any transition that is not purely instantaneous feedback.
